    • This project focuses on the design and implementation of an Online Application System Management for Scholarships and Business Permit, aimed at providing a centralized platform for students to apply for scholarships seamlessly.
    The system allows applicants to submit their credentials, track their application status, and receive notifications in real time.
    Simultaneously, it equips administrators with tools to manage applications efficiently, evaluate eligibility criteria, and maintain a structured database of applicants.


    Online application systems have emerged as an innovative solution.
    An online application system is a digital platform that enables applicants to submit forms, upload necessary documents, and track their application progress seamlessly through the internet.
    These systems also allow administrators to process and evaluate applications more efficiently, using automated workflows and centralized data management.
